Weather or Not

In this application called Weather or Not, a team of four developers worked to create a convenient and functional app that would encourage people to go outside and be active.

The apps capabilities include showing the daily weather, weekly weather, and a search bar for places near by.

Planning out a day has never been easier!

Taylor Swift Trivia

This app is a trivia game designed for people to test their knowledge of Taylor Swift. It is easily adaptable to other topics as well.

The apps capabilities include asking 8 questions, a timer from 20 seconds during each question, and summary page displaying answers correct, wrong, and unasnwered.

Try out your skills!

Word Guess Game

In this application you will find a word guess game similar to hangman. It is themed around animals.

The apps capabilities include showing you blanks for the mystery word, showing letters that are guessed, keeping track of wins/ losses, and displaying an animal image after each word guessed.

Can you guess the animal?

Train Schedule

In this application people are able to look up destinations to see when the next train will arrive.

The apps capabilities include inputting locations, keeping a schedule, and telling how many minutes away the next train is.

Build your train schedule now!
